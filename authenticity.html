AUTHENTICITY

At VinStreet, authenticity is our top priority. We source our clothing from reliable channels: sellers on Vinted, official suppliers, and large outlet stores. Every item is genuine, high-quality, and carefully checked, so our customers can be confident in their purchase.

Strict Inspection

Each garment is thoroughly examined by our experts, who check labels, stitching patterns, material quality, and design details. The unique wear patterns and natural aging of vintage pieces also play an important role in ensuring authenticity.

Why Authenticity Matters

Authenticity not only preserves the value of the item but also guarantees that you are wearing a genuine, true vintage piece. We want our customers to wear VinStreet clothing proudly, knowing that every piece is both stylish and authentic.

Our Promise

At VinStreet, every garment we sell is 100% authentic. Our reliable sources and expert inspections ensure that our customers always receive real, high-quality vintage items. For us, authenticity is not just a promise—it’s a guarantee.
