RETURNS

VinStreet sells only Grade A second-hand clothing (please refer to our “Sizing / Condition” page for more information about Grade A clothing).

We understand that when it comes to vintage clothing, especially pieces from the most prestigious and sought-after global brands, authenticity is paramount. Our reputation is built on the trust our customers place in us to deliver only genuine, high-quality vintage items, and we take this responsibility seriously.

When you shop at VinStreet, you can be confident that every item we sell is 100% authentic. We are committed to providing our customers not only with the best vintage clothing but also with the peace of mind that comes from knowing they are getting the real thing. From our rigorous authentication process to our partnerships with trusted third-party services, we take every step necessary to ensure that the items you purchase are as genuine as they are timeless.

In the world of vintage fashion, authenticity is more than just a label—it’s a promise. And at VinStreet, it’s a promise we stand by every day.

To comply with the Consumer Rights Act 2015, all garments are professionally laundered and pass through a strict quality assurance process. Garments are measured to give an accurate representation of their current size. For more information on sizing, please refer to our “Sizing / Condition” page.

We ensure that all garments are of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose, and as described. Fading, fabric wear, marks, and distressing may occur. Customers have the right to return faulty items.

We also sell mystery boxes where the garments are randomly selected by VinStreet, and we do our best to meet your style expectations.

The cost of returning non-faulty goods is the customer’s responsibility. In Romania, returns are free if made within 48 hours.
