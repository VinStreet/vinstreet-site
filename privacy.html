Privacy Policy

At VinStreet, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting your personal data and using it only in accordance with the applicable European Union data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when you place orders, register an account, subscribe to newsletters, or contact us. This may include your name, email, shipping address, payment details, and phone number.

We may also collect information via cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience on our site. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to process orders, manage accounts, provide customer service, analyze site usage, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Who We Share Information With

We may share your information with trusted partners to fulfill orders, including payment processors and delivery services. We may also share information if required by law or to prevent fraud or illegal activity.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may object to processing for marketing purposes, and you can withdraw consent at any time. Requests can be made via email to info@vinstreet.eu.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, the internet is not completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of data transmitted online.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for business, legal, or tax purposes. Generally, data is kept for up to 6 years for compliance with Romanian and EU regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Please review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
