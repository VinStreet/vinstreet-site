SIZING AND CONDITION

Sizing

Since all of our clothing is second-hand (vintage), garments may have lost their size tags or been shrunk or stretched over the years, so they may not fit true to their tagged size. To solve this, our team measures each garment and allocates a "fits as" size.

Our legwear is sized based on the original tagged size, which can be found on either the back tab (Levi's) or the inner care label.

When placing an order, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable fit.

Condition

We strictly supply Grade A vintage for all our regular boxes. Grade A vintage may show signs of age, such as fading, fabric wear, marks, and distressing.

All garments are professionally laundered and then pass through a strict quality assurance process, which includes authentication, condition checks, and sizing, before being picked and packed for orders.
